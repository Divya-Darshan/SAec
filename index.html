Preamble

S.A. Engineering College (Autonomous), Chennai – 600077, India is pleased to announce the 2026 International Conference on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Cyber Security (ICAMC 2026) to be held on 02–03 October 2026. This prestigious event aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers to discuss and disseminate innovations at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Cybersecurity. The conference will feature keynote addresses, technical paper presentations, workshops, and panel discussions from thought leaders and global experts.

About SAEC

S. A. Engineering College was established by the Dharma Naidu Educational and Charitable Trust in the year 1998-99. The college is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The college acquired the Autonomous status in the year 2019-20. The college maintains a high standard of Education by providing world-class academic facilities, highly qualified faculty, and a quality-driven environment. It is ISO 9001:2015 certified and accredited by NAAC and NBA with 'A' Grade. The college offers 11 UG and 6 PG programmes and has autonomous status for over 10 years.

About the Department of CSE (Cyber Security)

The Department of CSE (Cyber Security) is dedicated to delivering high-quality education and fostering innovation in the field of digital security. Established in 2023 with an intake of 60 students, the department is committed to shaping the next generation of cyber defenders for an increasingly digital and interconnected world. With a future-focused curriculum, the department emphasizes key domains such as Cyber Security, Software Security, Ethical Hacking, Machine Learning, Programming, and Data Structures. The program is designed around a flexible, choice-based learning model that integrates:
• Hands-on projects and practical labs
• Real-world problem-solving experiences
• Industry internships and exposure
• Development of essential soft skills and professional ethics
This holistic approach equips students with both technical expertise and the confidence to address contemporary cybersecurity challenges. The department actively promotes research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and continuous learning to prepare students for leadership roles in the ever-evolving field of cyber security.

About the Conference

The International Conference on Emerging Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Cyber Security aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and practitioners from across the globe to discuss recent advancements, emerging trends, and future directions in intelligent computing and secure digital systems. The conference serves as a premier interdisciplinary platform to exchange innovative ideas, present cutting-edge research, and explore AI-driven solutions for complex cyber and data-driven challenges in the digital era.

Key Highlights:

Intelligent Intrusion Detection Systems
ML & AI for Real-Time Threat Analytics
Machine Learning in Malware Classification
Federated Learning and Data Privacy
AI Applications in Cloud, Edge, and IoT Security
Ethical and Explainable AI for Trust and Transparency

Conference Details

Objectives

  1. To provide a global platform for showcasing emerging AI, ML, and Deep Learning techniques.
  2. To promote research and innovation in AI-driven cyber security solutions.
  3. To encourage collaboration between academia, industry, and research organizations.
  4. To explore future-ready intelligent and secure systems for real-world applications.
  5. To discuss ethical, trustworthy, and resilient AI models for secure digital ecosystems.

Outcomes

  1. Dissemination of high-quality research findings in AI, ML, DL, and Cyber Security.
  2. Identification of emerging research challenges and future directions.
  3. Development of industry-academia collaborations and research networks.
  4. Publication of peer-reviewed papers in reputed proceedings/journals.
  5. Enhanced knowledge sharing on secure, intelligent, and sustainable technologies.

Paper Submission Process

• Submissions must be original and unpublished work.

• Papers must be submitted via the conference submission portal: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/User/Login?

• Each submission will undergo double-blind peer review.

Conference Proceedings

All submitted papers will go through a plagiarism check process and would be peer reviewed. The papers recommended by the reviewers will be scheduled for presentation. However, it is mandatory that at least one of the authors would register for presenting the paper. The papers presented would be published in the conference proceedings, in print as well as electronic form.
